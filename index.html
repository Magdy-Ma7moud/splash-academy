About Us

Splash Academy is a dynamic sports academy dedicated to nurturing young athletes. We offer a wide range of sports activities for kids, including swimming, kickboxing, basketball, soccer, and gymnastics. Our programs are designed to develop skills, promote fitness, and instill a love for sports in a fun and supportive environment. At Splash Academy, we believe in fostering both physical and personal growth, ensuring that every child has the opportunity to shine.

Services We Provided

Here’s a brief description for each sport offered at Splash Academy

  • Swimming

    At Splash Academy, our swimming program is designed to build strong, confident swimmers from the ground up. Our certified instructors focus on teaching proper techniques, water safety, and endurance in a fun and encouraging environment. Whether your child is a beginner or an advanced swimmer, we tailor our lessons to meet their needs and help them excel.

  • Soccer

    Our soccer program emphasizes teamwork, agility, and strategic thinking. Kids will learn the fundamentals of the game, including dribbling, passing, shooting, and defensive skills, all while having fun on the field. Our coaches are dedicated to developing both the technical abilities and sportsmanship that soccer demands.

  • Kickboxing

    Kickboxing at Splash Academy is a high-energy program that combines fitness with self-discipline. Kids learn powerful kicks, punches, and defensive techniques, helping to build strength, flexibility, and confidence. Our experienced instructors ensure a safe environment where children can thrive and develop their martial arts skills.

  • Basketball

    Our basketball program is designed to inspire young athletes through skill development and teamwork. From dribbling and shooting to defensive strategies and game-play tactics, kids will receive comprehensive training that enhances their abilities on the court. We focus on building a strong foundation in the fundamentals while promoting a love for the game.

  • Gymnastics

    In our gymnastics program, children explore the exciting world of balance, strength, and flexibility. From basic tumbling to advanced routines, our coaches guide kids through a series of fun and challenging activities that build confidence and coordination. Whether your child is new to gymnastics or has previous experience, they’ll find a place to grow at Splash Academy.

Splash Camp

offers exciting camp experiences that cater to different interests and age groups, ensuring that every child has an unforgettable adventure.

Day Camp

Our Splash Day Camp is perfect for kids who want to enjoy a fun-filled day of sports, activities, and making new friends. Each day is packed with a variety of games, sports training, arts and crafts, and outdoor exploration, all under the guidance of our experienced counselors. Day campers return home in the evening with new skills, stories to tell, and a big smile on their face.

Overnight Scout Camp

For those looking for a bit more adventure, our Overnight Scout Camp is an incredible opportunity to experience the great outdoors. Kids will spend their days engaging in exciting activities like swimming, hiking, team-building challenges, and learning essential scouting skills. Evenings are filled with campfires, stargazing, and bonding with fellow campers. Our overnight camp is designed to foster independence, teamwork, and a deep appreciation for nature, all within a safe and supportive environment.
